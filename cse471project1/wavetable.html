Wavetable

Jesse Wolven

Description

Wavetable synthesis uses very short audio recordings consisting of one or a few waveforms of a sound. The effects on this include: Wave playback on demand from the sequencer, Envelope generation, Pitch, Attack and sustain waves, and Table cross-fading. All of these effects are all played sequentially in one audio clip.

Supported Grading Components:

  • 10 - Wave playback on demand from the sequencer
  • 20 - Envelope generation
  • 30 - Pitch
  • 40 - Attack and sustain waves OR loop points
  • 50 - Table cross-fading OR glissando
